X / TWITTER

Overview

Twitter, officially known as X since 2023, is a prominent social networking service that facilitates the sharing of short messages called tweets. Launched in July 2006 by Jack Dorsey, Noah Glass, Biz Stone, and Evan Williams, Twitter has evolved into one of the largest social media platforms globally, with millions of active users. From breaking news and political movements to celebrity interactions and marketing campaigns, Twitter has positioned itself as a hub of immediacy and influence.

Why X / Twitter?

  • Share and access instant news, live events, and trending topics.
  • Connect with individuals, professionals, and communities worldwide.
  • Promote products, build brand identity, and engage customers.
  • Drive campaigns and raise awareness for social and global issues.
  • Interact directly with followers through replies and messages.

Features

  • Tweets: Short posts of up to 280 characters, which can include text, images, videos, or polls.
  • Retweets and Replies: Allow users to share content or join conversations effortlessly.
  • Hashtags: Categorize topics for easy discovery and trend tracking.
  • Trending Topics: Highlight popular discussions tailored by region or interest.
  • Twitter Spaces: Live audio rooms enabling real-time discussions or interviews.
  • Trending Topics: Lists and Communities: Features to organize interests or connect with niche audiences.

How to Create a X Account:

  1. 1. Click the Create Account button or scan the QR code above.
  2. 2. Click Sign Up or “Join X” and enter your name, email, or phone number.
  3. 3. Create a password and verify your information (email/phone).
  4. 4. Choose a username (handle) with the “@” symbol.
  5. 5. Upload a profile photo and header image (optional).
  6. 6. Write a brief bio (optional).
  7. 7. Select interests for personalized suggestions.
  8. 8. Follow accounts and customize privacy and notification settings.
  9. 8. Agree to terms and complete setup.
