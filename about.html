OUR TIMELINE

2004

Facebook was founded, revolutionising social networking and changing the way we connect online.

2005

YouTube launched, becoming the largest video-sharing platform and a go-to source for entertainment, tutorials, and vlogs.

2006

Twitter launched, offering a microblogging platform with real-time updates and trending topics.

2008

Spotify launched, transforming the music streaming industry by offering access to millions of songs.

2009

WhatsApp is a messaging app with end-to-end encryption, becoming a global communication tool.

2010

Instagram launched as a photo-sharing app, later expanding to Stories, Reels, and Shopping.

2010

Pinterest was introduced as a visual discovery platform, allowing users to pin images and ideas for inspiration.

2011

Snapchat introduced disappearing messages and stories, revolutionizing the way we share moments.

2016

TikTok (formerly Musical.ly) launched, gaining massive popularity with short-form videos and viral trends.

2021

Threads launched as a new social media platform by Meta (Facebook) focused on text-based conversations and real-time engagement.
