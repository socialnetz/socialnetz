How to Create a Facebook Account:

  1. 1. Click the Create Account button or scan the QR code above.
  2. 2. Enter your first and last name in the provided fields.
  3. 3. Provide your email address or mobile phone number.
  4. 4. Create a secure password for your account.
  5. 5. Select your date of birth using the dropdown menus.
  6. 6. Choose your gender.
  7. 7. Click the Sign Up button to complete registration.
  8. 8. Check your email or phone for a confirmation code and verify your account.

Your Facebook account is now ready!
