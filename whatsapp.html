WHATSAPP

Overview

WhatsApp is a messaging platform that allows users to send text messages, voice messages, images, videos, and make voice and video calls. Founded in 2009 by Jan Koum and Brian Acton, WhatsApp has grown to become one of the most popular communication tools globally, with billions of active users. It is owned by Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook), and is known for its end-to-end encryption, making it a secure and reliable way to communicate.
