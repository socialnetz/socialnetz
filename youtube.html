YOUTUBE

Overview

YouTube is a prominent online video-sharing platform founded in February 2005 by Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karim. Acquired by Google in 2006 for approximately $1.65 billion, YouTube allows users to upload, view, and share videos across a diverse range of genres, including music, vlogs, tutorials, and entertainment. With over 2.7 billion monthly active users as of January 2024, the platform facilitates significant user interaction through likes, comments, and subscriptions. It also offers monetization options for creators through advertisements, enabling many to earn revenue from their content.
