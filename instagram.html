INSTAGRAM

Overview

Instagram is a photo and video-sharing social networking service founded by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger in 2010. Originally designed as a mobile app for photo sharing, Instagram quickly gained a massive following and expanded into a platform for sharing stories, reels, and IGTV videos. With over a billion active users, Instagram has become one of the most popular social media platforms worldwide, connecting people through images, videos, and live streams.

Global Impact

Instagram has transformed how people interact and share visual content globally. It has empowered small businesses, fostered influencer culture, and provided a platform for creative expression. The introduction of Instagram Live and Reels has changed the social media landscape for the better.
