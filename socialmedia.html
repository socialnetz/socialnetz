What is Social Networking?

Social networking is the use of digital platforms or websites to create and maintain relationships, interact with others, and share information in various forms such as text, images, videos, or links. It has revolutionized the way people connect, communicate, and collaborate globally, transcending geographical and cultural barriers. Social networking platforms serve as virtual hubs where individuals and organizations come together to exchange ideas, build communities, and create opportunities for personal, social, and professional growth.
