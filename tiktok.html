Features

  • Duet: A split screen feature that lets you combine your video with another video.
  • TikTok Shop: A built-in commerce feature that lets sellers, creators, affiliates sell directly to customers.
  • TikTok Sounds: A library of music and sound effects that you can use as background music in your videos.
  • Voiceovers: A tool that lets you record voiceovers to add narration to your videos.

Global Impact

TikTok's diverse content ecosystem includes popular niches such as music, fitness, beauty, education, and gaming, which cater to a wide range of audiences.
